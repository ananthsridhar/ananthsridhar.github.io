About

Ananth is the sort of person that gets excited when something has an API and has developer OCD about his code. Ananth is also the sort of person that loves Bajji, Vada Pav, Dogs and talking about himself in third person. Loves to immerse himself in code during his free time and thinking about very deep things while listening to random Spotify playlists.

Meanwhile, he has a lot of places on his travel bucket list, ticking them off slowly, but surely. Maybe he'll blog about it someday

Thats My Station [Under Development]

A personal project as well as a learning experience, I set out to develop a React native application that can prevent that constant commute fear of missing your station. Using your location and indexed station data, it can sound an alarm, alerting you to your approaching destination station. I must admit that this idea was born out of multiple frustrating personal experiences of the kind that I set out to prevent.
